Magazine Spread

When Women Ruled the Tech World

I created this project in my very first Visual Communication Design course in Fall 2017. The goal of this final project was to develop a magazine spread that was related to a passion or interest. I personally am very passionate about equality among genders and I wanted to specifically pin point the drastic waves in which women take place in the tech world. I used a bold yellow to emphasize the power and importance women held, and developed a spread that highlights the dramatic changes in their engagement and social acceptance of their position among technology.
